COMPANY

Founded in 2014 we are proud to be young aggressive company that delivered to our clients worldwide turn key solutions thinking outside the box. Working with the best in the industry we deliver to our clients the right solution, at the right price which resulted on repeated business for the last 4 years. Our turn key solutions and one stop shop to our clients means providing a full suite of services from full architectural design and engineering solutions for ballistics systems, containment and detailing to construction supervision and management. We also provide all range fit-out components, including simulation systems, targetry, anti-ricochet materials, bullet traps, and complete indoor range systems’ solutions. We have deployed indoor and outdoor shooting ranges and training systems for our clients around the globe.
